    Spotify Ad Campaign

    For this spec ad campaign, I wanted to create some visually basic ads that would generate new paid subscriptions for Spotify. These ads focused on appealing to potential customers that love making playlists. Creating and sharing playlists is one of the more social and appealing parts of the app. It's nice to share a playlist with a friend or significant other and it's also nice to look back on playlists and get a peek into how your life was at that time. Music says so much about a person and a playlist provides a snapshot.
