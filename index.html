    Canadian Ad Campaign for Blundstone

    In this spec ad campaign, I wrote several ads that focus exclusively on Canadian audiences. Canada is an ideal market to sell boots, but many Canadians already own Blundstones making it difficult to find new buyers. These ads will remind buyers that Blundstone builds boots that can stand up to even the harshest Canadian winters.

    Product Advantages: Practical, waterproof, durable, timeless style and design.

    Product Disadvantages: Expensive, market is already saturated with Blundstone owners, brand not necessarily associated with Winter boots.

    Additional Campaign Ideas: Have product teams on Canadian streets with the boots and allow prospective buyers to test the boots in puddles, slush and snow.

    Give out free Blundstone branded socks to prospective buyers in case their feet get wet in their current boots. Encourage buyers to consider Blundstone Winter boots for their next purchase.

    Give out free Blundstone laces to prospective buyers, so that they can replace the laces on their current boots and remind buyers that Blundstones have no laces and still offer a comfortable and supportive fit.

    Blundstone’s Winter Boot Collection

    There’s so much more to Canadian winters than just frostbite and 4:30 PM sunsets. With so much to see and do this winter Blundstones will get you outside and embracing the winter season. Forget about hibernating and get out there. Whether hiking the Pacific Rim National Park or checking out the latest Toronto shops on Queen Street Blundstones will keep you warm and dry all winter long.

    Our winter boots are made to overcome the most rainy, slushy and frozen parts of Canadian winter. Blundstone boots feature:

    • Genuine Shearling Wool Footbeds
    • Thinsulate insulation
    • Waterproof Leather
    • Thermo-urethane Outsole
    • XRD Heel Pads

    Winter is tough, but with Blundstones you will be able to take on everything winter throws your way. And if your Blundstones get a little roughed up we will repair or replace them no questions asked. So get out there and enjoy winter this year!
