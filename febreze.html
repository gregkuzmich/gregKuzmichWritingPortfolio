    Febreze Ad Campaign

    In these spec ads I created a headline driven campaign that focuses on the social embarrassment of using the bathroom and how Febreze can do more than cover the aftermath. Febreze's extra strength formula eliminates the smells at the source and eliminates that self-conscious feeling. In the end no one wants to be known as the person that stinks up a bathroom. It's embarrassing and a social faux pas whether at an in-law's house or girlfriend's new apartment, but with Febreze Extra Strength you can eliminate the smells right at the source and eliminate any worry about what someone else might be walking into.

    Product Advantages: An already recognizable and dominant brand for odour elimination, affordable, trusted and works by eliminating odours instead of masking them.

    Product Disadvantages: Seen as chemical or unnatural, scent not enjoyed by all users, too strong smelling and seen as cheap and tacky.

    Additional Campaign Ideas:

    Give out free mini canisters to people about to enter public washrooms.

    Have a scent challenge that shows how effective Febreze is at eliminating odours by having product demonstrations that cover up other run of the day smelly items. For example, hockey equipment, running shoes or an area where a wet dog has been.
