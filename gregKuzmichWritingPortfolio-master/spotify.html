    Spotify Ad Campaign

    For this spec ad campaign, I wanted to create some visually basic ads that would generate new paid subscriptions for Spotify. These ads focused on appealing to potential customers that love making playlists. It's nice to share a playlist with a friend or significant other and it feels good to look back on old playlists and see who we once were musically. Music says so much about a person and a playlist provides a snapshot.

    Product Advantages: One of the most popular apps for music, a large user base, a large amount of customaization within the app and ability to share via social media.

    Product Disadvantages: A saturated market with a high number of paid subscribers, direct competition with Apple Music and little to distinguish itself from Apple Music.

    Additional Campaign Ideas:

    Prommote the sharing of playlists with friends, family and significant others.

    Have playlist competitions for different genres and cities.
