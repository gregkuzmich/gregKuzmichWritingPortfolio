    National Alliance to End Homelessness Ad Campaign

    For this spec ad campaign, I wanted to focus on the improvisation needed to overcome the lack of necessities for homeless populations. Obviously one of the first things that comes to mind when thinking of the homeless is lack of housing, but there are so many other issues that come with it. The lack of necessities such as shampoo, toothpaste, feminine hygiene products and so much more makes being homeless even more unbearable. This ad campaign highlights how the nameless and overlooked homeless populations struggle to find access to the very basics that we take for granted.
