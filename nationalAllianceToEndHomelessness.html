    National Alliance to End Homelessness Ad Campaign

    For this spec ad campaign, I wanted to focus on the need for improvisation within homeless populations when trying to acquire the basic necessities. The lack of shampoo, blankets, feminine hygiene products and many other day-to-day items makes being homeless even more unbearable.

    Product Advantages: A noble cause, homelessness affects all communities, seeing indignity in fellow humans creates empathy and compels people to donate.

    Product Challenges: Misconceptions regarding homelessness, fear of the homeless, many see the homeless as deserving of their status.

    Additional Campaign Ideas:

    Have National Alliance to End Homelessness branded stickers to stick to items that the homeless use for alternative purposes. For example, a sticker could state “This is a Bed” and be stuck on a park bench.

    Create community fundraisers, sponsored by the National Alliance to End Homelessness, to raise donations of everyday items such as toothbrushes, socks, tampons and soap for local homelessness initiatives instead of just money or non-perishable food items.
